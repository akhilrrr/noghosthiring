Transparency in Progress


NoGhostHiring is dedicated to fairness, accountability, and accuracy. This page gives companies a way to submit clarifications, corrections, or context about their hiring practices. By providing a transparent channel, we make sure our platform shares information responsibly, maintains trust for both candidates and companies, and protects sensitive data at every step.

Who Can Submit

Only companies and recruiters can submit requests for clarification or context about reports published on NoGhostHiring. Candidates or other external parties cannot use this page to submit requests.

Transparency Submission Form ↗

Submissions may include:

  • Correcting any factual errors in reports to make sure the information is accurate and fair
  • Providing additional context about a company’s hiring practices so candidates understand the full picture
  • Sharing public clarifications on processes that may have caused confusion or misunderstanding, giving both candidates and companies transparency

All submissions undergo moderation and verification to ensure fairness.

Verification Process

Every submission is carefully reviewed by the NoGhostHiring moderation team before it appears publicly. This verification process makes sure that:

  1. Authenticity: Every submission must reflect a real company and a genuine experience from a candidate.
  2. Data Safety: No information that could identify a candidate is ever shared, displayed, or exposed publicly.
  3. Neutrality: Company clarifications are presented clearly and objectively, without editorial interpretation or bias.

Once a submission is verified, its status is updated, and a neutral summary may be published for public viewing. Submissions that are unverified or rejected remain private, though they are recorded internally for review and quality control.
