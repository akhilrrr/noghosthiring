Nature of Service

NoGhostHiring turns experiences reported by candidates into structured data and meaningful insights. The platform is informational, not accusatory. It is not an employment service, a recruitment marketplace, or a verification authority. What we share are community-driven signals designed to help participants see patterns and trends. The platform exists in the public space: part collective memory, part accountability tool, and part open dataset.

Transparency Is Not Defamation

Publishing anonymized reports, aggregated scores, and structured data is not meant to declare guilt or prove a fact. It reflects the experiences shared by the community, carefully moderated for safety, fairness, and clarity. Transparency is about public insight and accountability. Defamation requires falsehood and malicious intent, and NoGhostHiring is a neutral platform where candidate reports and company clarifications can exist side by side. Readers are encouraged to interpret the information responsibly and thoughtfully.

NoGhostHiring does not erase history, but it allows context to grow and evolve over time.

Takedown Requests & Sensitive Content
